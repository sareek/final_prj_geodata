Stakeholders

The stakeholders for the website include researchers and practitioners trying to study problems related to air pollution in smart cities. People who are interest in these kinds of study are encourage to reach out to us through linkedIn. We also plan to create facebook group and held live sessions from time to time inviting data scientists, researchers and other practitioners to discuss relevant problems and find ideas for solutions to those problems.

If you have any questions/ideas relevant to the study feel free to drop an email at sdhungel@gmail.com
